AR is for Everyone!

A Community, Creative, and Collective Space

The AR is a project conceived by a group of people who believe in the power of community and collaboration. Our goal is to create a space accessible to all, where you can do various things:

Features

  • Work

    The AR offers a calm and inspiring environment to work, with free Wi-Fi, comfortable desks, and chairs.

  • Meet

    Whether for a business meeting, a gathering of friends, or a workshop, the AR has a versatile and well-equipped meeting room.

  • Rehearse

    Musicians, artists, and theater groups can use the AR's rehearsal room to practice and prepare their performances.

  • Learn and Share

    The AR offers a space for classes, meetings, workshops, talks, and mentoring on a wide range of topics.

  • Exhibit

    Visual artists can exhibit their work in the AR's exhibition room, giving visibility to their art.

  • Watch

    The AR also functions as a cinema, where small screenings and film exhibitions are held.

  • Promote and Exchange Ideas

    The AR is a space to share ideas and projects, promoting collaboration among community members.

How it Works

The AR operates by reservation and on a donation and/or exchange system. We believe that accessibility is fundamental to building a strong and vibrant community. Therefore, we want the AR to be a space accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial capacity.

Showcases

The AR also has 2 showcases available for artistic installations. If you have an installation proposal that contains a visible desire to communicate with the outside world, please contact us!
